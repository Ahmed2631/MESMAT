About MESMAT

MESMAT Company is a leading Saudi company founded in 2015 in Riyadh, to be a strategic partner in providing integrated solutions that cover several vital sectors.

Our Story

Building the Future Together

MESMAT was established in 2015 in Riyadh as a Saudi brand aimed at elevating the standards of supplying the hospitality and hotel sector.

Since its inception, MESMAT has gained recognition in its field, becoming one of the most experienced Saudi companies in supplying the tourism and hotel sector. Through providing high-quality products, we have earned the trust of various clients in the hotel sector, both within and outside the Kingdom.

With the launch of Vision 2030 in 2016, we found our aspirations aligning with the vision, contributing to the advancement of tourism in the Kingdom and the enhancement of the hospitality and hotel sector.

Our Purpose

Vision

We firmly believe that the Kingdom stands as one of the richest and most alluring destinations for global tourists, boasting a profound history, rich cultural heritage, and significant economic stature.

Mission

Hence, it is our duty to elevate tourism in our Kingdom by delivering the highest quality products, especially tailored for our clients in the hospitality sector within the Kingdom and the wider region.

Commitment

Our aspiration is to forge partnerships with the world's leading brands, ensuring the provision of exceptional products that cater to the unique needs of our distinguished clients in the hospitality sector.

Our Values

Innovation

Developing modern and unconventional solutions that align with the aspirations of the Saudi market and resonate with global changes.

Partnership

Building strong relationships with clients and partners based on trust, transparency, and mutual benefit.

Excellence

Commitment to applying the highest quality standards in every product and service, to ensure true value and an exceptional experience.

Sustainability

Contributing to achieving the objectives of the Kingdom's Vision 2030 by supporting economic diversification.
